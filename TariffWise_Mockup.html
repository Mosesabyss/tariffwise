Annotation key

1The user enters what they know. The AI fills the gaps in the next step through targeted questions.
2Footwear is one of the highest-risk categories for misclassification, so it anchors the worked example.
3The AI asks only the questions that change the code: upper material, ankle height, toe construction.
4The interview mirrors how a customs expert interrogates a product to find its essential character.
5The user can interrogate the result in plain language. This builds trust and surfaces the reasoning.
6The disclaimer keeps the tool within permissible decision support. It suggests and explains. It does not file the entry.
7Every classification and its reasoning is stored, creating the record that reasonable care requires.
8An exportable record defends the classification if customs audits the entry. This is the core point of differentiation.
